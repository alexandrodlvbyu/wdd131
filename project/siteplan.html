Climatic Trends - Site Plan

Site Name

Climatic Trends is the name of the site. It reflects the main objective: providing a platform for exploring and analyzing global climate trends. The name was chosen for its simplicity and clarity while remaining memorable.

Optional domain (not required): climatictrends.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to allow users to explore climate trends in various cities worldwide. We will provide climate data, city comparisons, and information on climate change awareness.

Scenarios

  • How can I compare the climate trends of two different cities?
  • What is the current climate data for a specific city?

Color Scheme

The site will use the following colors:

  • Green (#4CAF50): Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for section backgrounds and data cards.

Typography

The selected fonts for the site are:

  • Arial, sans-serif for the main text and paragraphs.
  • Roboto, sans-serif for headings and subheadings.

Wireframe

The wireframe of the site will feature a column layout with a navigation menu on the left and the main content on the right. The design will be responsive, adjusting to different screen sizes, including mobile phones and wider desktop screens.
